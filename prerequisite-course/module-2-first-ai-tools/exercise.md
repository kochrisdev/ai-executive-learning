# Module 2 Exercise — Side-by-Side Tool Comparison

**Time:** ~10 minutes
**Submit to:** Your facilitator or learning portal before the Deep Dive

---

## Instructions

Ask the same business question to ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. Use a real question relevant to your work but non-confidential.

**Suggested question format:**
```
What are the most important trends in [your industry] in 2026 that
a senior executive should be aware of, and what are the strategic
implications for a mid-sized company?
```

Or use your own question.
