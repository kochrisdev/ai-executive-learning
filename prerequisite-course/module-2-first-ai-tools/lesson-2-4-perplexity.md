# Lesson 2.4 — Perplexity: AI-Powered Research

**Time:** ~15 minutes

---

## Why Perplexity Changes Executive Research

Traditional research workflow: Google → click links → read articles → synthesize manually → 45 minutes.

Perplexity workflow: ask question → get synthesized answer with cited sources → verify top claims → 5 minutes.

Perplexity is not a chatbot. It is a **search engine with AI synthesis**. Every answer includes live citations, so you can click through to verify. This makes it far more trustworthy than ChatGPT for research where recency and accuracy matter.

### New in 2026: Perplexity Deep Research

Perplexity now offers a **Deep Research** mode that:
- Spends 2–5 minutes running dozens of searches autonomously
- Synthesizes findings into a comprehensive, cited report
- Comparable to what a junior analyst would produce in half a day

**Executive use case:** Before a board meeting on a new market, run a Deep Research query — you receive a 5-10 page cited report on the competitive landscape, key players, regulatory environment, and recent moves.

## Interface Walkthrough

- **Search bar:** Type your question in natural language
- **Focus options:** All / Academic / Writing / Wolfram Alpha / YouTube / Reddit — use "Academic" for research papers, "Reddit" for user sentiment
- **Follow-up questions:** Perplexity suggests follow-ups automatically
- **Sources panel:** Every answer cites its sources on the right — always review these

## The Best Use Cases for Executives

### Competitive Intelligence
```
What are the 3 most significant strategic moves [Competitor Name]
has made in the last 12 months? Include funding, product launches,
and executive hires.
```

### Market Landscape
```
Summarize the current state of the [your industry] market in 2026.
Include key growth drivers, headwinds, and emerging disruptors.
```

### Regulatory Monitoring
```
What new regulations affecting [your sector] have been enacted or
proposed in the EU and US in the past 6 months?
```

### Pre-Meeting Briefing
```
I'm meeting with the CEO of [Company Name] tomorrow.
Give me: their recent news, strategic priorities, any public challenges,
and 5 smart questions I could ask.
```

### Technology Evaluation
```
Compare Make.com, Zapier, and n8n for enterprise workflow automation.
Include pricing, integration depth, and best-fit use cases.
```

## Evaluating Perplexity Sources

Perplexity is only as good as its sources. Train yourself to:

1. Check the source list — are these credible outlets or random blogs?
2. Click through on any claim that will influence a decision
3. Check the date — is the information current?
4. Note when Perplexity flags uncertainty ("According to some sources...")

**Rule:** For any Perplexity output that informs a financial, legal, or strategic decision — verify at least the top 2 sources directly.

## Key Takeaway

Replace your default Google research habit with Perplexity for business questions. You will get synthesized, cited answers in a fraction of the time. Maintain the verification habit — the synthesis is fast, but the judgment is still yours.
