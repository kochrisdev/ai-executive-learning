# Lesson 2.1 — Setting Up Your AI Stack

**Time:** ~20 minutes

---

## Step 1: Create Your Accounts

Complete each of the following in order. Use your work email where possible.

### ChatGPT (OpenAI)
1. Go to [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com)
2. Click "Sign Up" → use your work email
3. Upgrade to ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) to access GPT-4o
4. In Settings → Data Controls: disable "Improve the model for everyone" to prevent your conversations from being used in training

### Claude (Anthropic)
1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
2. Click "Sign Up" → use your work email
3. Upgrade to Claude Pro ($20/month) for extended context (200K tokens — handles entire documents)
4. Anthropic does not use Pro conversation data for training by default

### Perplexity
1. Go to [perplexity.ai](https://perplexity.ai)
2. Sign in with Google or create an account
3. Free tier is sufficient for this course; Pro ($20/month) adds more searches and GPT-4 access

## Step 3: Data Privacy — What to Enter and What Not To

### Safe to enter into consumer AI tools:
- General questions and hypothetical scenarios
- Publicly available information
- Draft text using fictional names/companies
- Your own thoughts and ideas (non-confidential)

### Never enter into consumer AI tools:
- Customer names, emails, or account details
- Financial data, forecasts, or internal P&L
- Legal strategy, litigation details, or attorney-client communications
- Employee personal information
- Unreleased product plans or IP
- Credentials, passwords, or API keys

### The Enterprise Alternative
If your organization has **Microsoft Copilot** (M365 license) or **Google Workspace AI**, those tools keep your data within your organization's tenant. Prefer those for sensitive work.

---

## Step 4: Check Your Company's AI Policy

Before using AI tools for work tasks, answer these questions:

- [ ] Does our organization have an official AI usage policy?
- [ ] Which tools are approved for business use?
- [ ] Is there an enterprise tier of any tool already licensed?
- [ ] Who is the internal point of contact for AI tools questions?

If you don't know the answers, check with your IT or Legal team before proceeding with work-sensitive content.
