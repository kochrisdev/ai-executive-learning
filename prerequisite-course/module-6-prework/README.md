# Module 6: Pre-Work Assignment

**Duration:** ~30 minutes
**Due:** 48 hours before your Deep Dive session
**Purpose:** Ensure facilitators can tailor the Deep Dive to the specific needs, tools, and problems your cohort brings.

---

## What to Submit

Complete the [Pre-Work Assignment](./prework-assignment.md) and submit it to your facilitator.

---

## Completion Checklist

Before the Deep Dive, confirm you have:

- [ ] Completed Modules 1–5 (all lessons and exercises)
- [ ] Submitted all 5 module exercises
- [ ] Completed and submitted the Pre-Work Assignment
- [ ] Created accounts in ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, and Perplexity
- [ ] Identified a real business problem or document to work on during live exercises
- [ ] Reviewed your organization's AI usage policy (or noted the absence of one)
- [ ] Completed your AI Opportunity Canvas (Module 5 exercise)
