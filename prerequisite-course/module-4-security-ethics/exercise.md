# Module 4 Exercise — Draft Your Team's AI Usage Guideline

**Time:** ~5 minutes (using AI to assist)
**Submit to:** Your facilitator or learning portal before the Deep Dive

---

## Instructions

Use the following prompt in ChatGPT or Claude to generate a first draft of an AI usage policy for your team. Then review and edit it.

---

## Step 1: Run This Prompt

```
You are an experienced Chief Information Security Officer and AI governance expert.

Draft a one-page AI Usage Guideline for a team of [number] people in a [your industry] company.
The team's function is: [e.g., sales, finance, marketing, product, operations]

The policy should cover:
1. Approved AI tools and tiers
2. Data classification rules (what can go into which tools)
3. Output verification requirements
4. Human-in-the-loop requirements for consequential decisions
5. Where to report questions or incidents

Tone: Clear, practical, and team-friendly. Not a legal document.
Format: Single page, readable by a non-technical team member.
Avoid: Dense legal language, vague prohibitions, or anything that sounds like it was written by a committee.
```

---

## Step 2: Review and Edit the Draft

Check each section:

- [ ] Approved tools list is accurate for your organization
- [ ] Data rules are specific enough to be actionable
- [ ] Verification requirements are realistic (not too burdensome)
- [ ] Human-in-the-loop rules cover your team's highest-risk use cases
- [ ] Reporting channel is a real person or team, not a generic address

---

## Step 3: Reflect

1. What surprised you about building this policy — what was harder or easier than expected?

2. What would you add or change for your specific team context?

3. Would you be comfortable distributing this to your team tomorrow? What's missing?

---
