## Why Strategy Before Tools

Executives who jump straight to tools without a strategic frame end up with:
- Tool sprawl with no coherent stack
- Impressive demos that never scale to team adoption
- ROI conversations they cannot answer
- Boards asking "what's your AI strategy?" with no confident answer

This module gives you the frame before the Deep Dive gives you the tools.
