## Why Strategy Before Tools

Impact-makers who jump straight to tools without a strategic frame often end up with:
- Tool sprawl with no coherent focus on actual impact goals
- Impressive demos that never translate into time saved for communities
- Difficulty explaining AI adoption to donors, partners, or board members
- Technology that doesn't serve the people it's meant to help

This module gives you the strategic frame before the Innovation Studio Deep Dive gives you the tools. The goal is not to use AI for its own sake — it is to use AI to do more of the work that creates positive change in Myanmar.
