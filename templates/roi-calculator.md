## Section 1: Efficiency ROI

### Time Savings Calculation

| Input | Your Value |
|---|---|
| → Number of people in scope | |
| → Average hours saved per person per week | |
| → Fully-loaded hourly cost per person ($) | |
| Weeks per year | 52 |
| **Annual Efficiency Value** | **= People × Hrs × Cost × 52** |

**Example:** 10 people × 5 hrs × $150/hr × 52 = **$390,000/year**
