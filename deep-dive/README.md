# AI Tools Deep Dive — Workshop Guide

> A 2–3 day intensive workshop for executives who have completed the Prerequisite Course. Every session produces a real, deployable output.

---

## Prerequisites

All participants must have completed the [Prerequisite Course](../prerequisite-course/) before attending. Facilitators should confirm completion 48 hours before the workshop.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Every session produces an output** — no theoretical exercises without a real deliverable
2. **Use your own problems** — exercises use material participants brought from their real work
3. **Peer learning** — cohort shares prompts, outputs, and approaches; the group learns faster together
4. **Facilitator demonstrates first** — all tools are demonstrated live before participants try them
5. **Done is better than perfect** — the goal is deployable, not polished

## Materials Needed

- [ ] Laptop per participant (tablet not sufficient)
- [ ] Active accounts: ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, Perplexity, Make.com (free), Fireflies (free)
- [ ] Chrome browser with Wispr Flow extension installed
- [ ] Strong WiFi (AI tools are bandwidth-intensive with multiple users)
- [ ] Each participant's Pre-Work Assignment (submitted in advance)
- [ ] Printed or digital copies of prompt templates from `/templates`
