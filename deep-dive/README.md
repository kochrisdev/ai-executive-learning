# Innovation Studio Deep Dive
### DreamLabs Foundation — AI Mastery Workshop

> *2.5 days of hands-on AI skill-building for impact-makers. Every session produces a real output you can deploy for your venture, community, or initiative.*

---

## Prerequisites

All Fellows must have completed the [AI Foundations Course](../prerequisite-course/) before attending. Facilitators confirm completion 48 hours before the workshop.

---

## Workshop Purpose

The Innovation Studio Deep Dive is where learning becomes action. Fellows arrive having completed the foundations course; they leave with:

- A personal AI tool stack configured for their work
- A working custom AI assistant built for their initiative
- At least one automated workflow running
- A 90-day AI adoption plan, peer-reviewed by the cohort
- New peers from across Myanmar's impact-maker community

## DreamLabs Innovation Studio Principles

1. **Every session produces an output** — no theoretical exercises without a real deliverable
2. **Use your own challenges** — all exercises use material Fellows brought from their real initiatives
3. **Fellows learn from Fellows** — the cohort shares prompts, outputs, and approaches; collective learning accelerates everyone
4. **Mentors demonstrate first** — all tools are demonstrated live before Fellows try them
5. **Impact over polish** — the goal is deployable and useful, not perfect
