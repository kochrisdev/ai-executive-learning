**Day:** Day 3, Morning
**Duration:** 3 hours
**Facilitator:** Lead instructor
**Key output:** Each participant presents their 90-day AI adoption plan

- Know how to evaluate AI vendors and make informed investment decisions

- Have a peer-reviewed, actionable plan they can implement immediately

## Exercise 1: AI Business Case Draft (25 min)

Participants use their AI Opportunity Canvas (from Module 5) to draft a 1-page business case.

**Structure:**
1. The Opportunity (what problem we're solving and why now)
2. Proposed Approach (tools, timeline, team)
3. Expected ROI (efficiency hours × cost rate + any revenue impact)
4. Investment Required (tool costs + training time + IT setup)
5. Risk Mitigation (data governance, change management)
6. Success Metrics (what we measure and when)

**Use this prompt in ChatGPT:**
```
You are a management consultant building an AI investment business case.

Using the following context, draft a concise 1-page executive summary
that I can present to our leadership team:

Company context: [describe]
Opportunity: [paste from AI Opportunity Canvas]
Proposed tools: [list]
Expected time savings: [hours × people × cost]
Timeline: [90 days to X result]

Format: Short paragraphs, punchy. End with a clear Ask (budget/approval needed).
```

## Exercise 2: 90-Day Plan Presentation (5 min per participant)

Each participant presents their plan using this structure:

1. **My AI North Star** (1 sentence — the outcome you're driving toward)
2. **The 3 use cases I'm prioritizing** (and why)
3. **The tools I'm deploying** (specific tools, not categories)
4. **My 90-day milestones** (Week 2, Week 4, Week 8, Week 12)
5. **How I'll measure success** (specific metrics)
6. **My one biggest risk** and how I'll mitigate it
7. **What I need from this cohort** — one specific ask

## Accountability Pairing — The DreamLabs Way

The DreamLabs Foundation is built on the power of community and peer support. At the close of the program:
- Each Fellow is paired with one peer from the cohort
- Pairs commit to a 30-day check-in call
- Each pair shares: What did you deploy? What impact did it create? What's blocking you? What do you need?
- Pairs also celebrate wins — document a real impact story to share back with the DreamLabs community

Exchange contact information and schedule the call before leaving. These peer relationships often become some of the most valuable connections in a Fellow's DreamLabs journey.

## Facilitator Notes

- The reflections opening on Day 3 is important — it surfaces surprises, reinforces learning, and transitions energy from "learning mode" to "planning mode"
- Business case draft exercise: some executives will want to skip this — emphasize that the ROI framing is what turns the Deep Dive into organizational change, not just personal learning
- 90-day plan presentations: keep time discipline (5 min each), but allow 2–3 minutes for cohort questions — the peer feedback is often the most valuable part
- Accountability pairing: this is optional but significantly improves 90-day follow-through — strongly encourage it
- Close the program on energy: acknowledge what the group has accomplished in 2–3 days, name the specific outputs produced, and reinforce that the real learning starts now
